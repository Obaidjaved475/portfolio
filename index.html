About Me

I am award-winning Webflow Developer specialized in UX/UI, currently living in Lahore, Pak. Where I have been working as a freelancer for about 4 years.

I work from the user experience to the visual details and then transform these in working code. I am a minimal designer and I really like the simple and elegant style. I worked with a range of clients focusing on web apps and platforms with Google in partnership with Webflow.
